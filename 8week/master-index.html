Week 2

Keeping Active


Tips for keeping Active

Being active may be different for different people, however, it's important to be active in some way or another every day. Whether it’s getting out for a walk around the block or playing a team sport, joining a gym or small group classes, there are a variety of ways to fill up your exercise plan with different activities for the week.

High Energy Food

I've chosen High Energy food this week to support our keeping active focus. Food is our body’s fuel and without the right fuel, you will find it hard to keep up with your training. Fueling our bodies with the right fuel at the right time of the day will give us the energy we may have never known before.


Warmup

The warm-up before you exercise is super important as it gently prepares the body for exercises by gradually increasing the heart rate and circulation, this will loosen the joints and increase blood flow to the muscles.

At Home Workout

Completing each exercise for 10 repetitions each, then moving to the next exercise with little to no rest in between. When you get to the end of the list of exercises, start from the start again trying to get through as many rounds within the 5 minutes as you can. Try to beat your score during the 5 weeks. Once you reach at least 3 rounds move to the next level.

Pre and post workout Snacks

Sometimes you can find it hard to eat before going to the gym or working out, and in some cases, can’t stand the thought of it afterward. However, just like breakfast, these meals are just as important, the pre-snack, fueling our bodies with energy and the post-snack aids in the body’s recovery.

Feeling Good

Feeling Good While Losing Weight

Losing excess weight is one of the hardest things you will do, just because it's hard doesn't mean it can’t be done. Below are a few tips to help you be successful.

Feel Good Food

I've chosen these recipes for you to start with because we all know how hard it is to start eating healthy. These recipes are sure to please as they are fast and easy to prepare and can be made ahead of time to grab on the way out the door or even as a sweet treat after a meal.


Exercise Planning

Working out with consistency is important for achieving fitness results. Creating a workout plan will help you to achieve greater consistency. A commitment to a regular workout will increase your fitness level, improve your health and generate a greater sense of mental wellbeing.

Food Plan & Tracking Journal

Use this food tracking Diary to record what you are eating then how you are feeling after you have eaten. Were you starving before your next scheduled meal, or did you feel sick because you ate too much?

Your Daily Water Goal

It might come as a shock to you at how much water your body needs, you may think its impossible to reach that amount of water on a daily basis. Just like everything else the more you try to reach the target the closer you will become.
