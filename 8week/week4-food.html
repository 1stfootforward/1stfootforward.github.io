Low-Calorie Meals

By choosing some low-calorie options for your different meal times will free up calories to use later. Like if you are going out for dinner and drinks you could have a low-calorie breakfast and lunch so you don’t go over. Give these recipes below ago, and see for yourself just because it's low in calories doesn’t mean you have to give up on the flavour!

Spinach Fritters

Great snack you can grab on the go for breakfast or lunch.


  • ½ bag baby spinach
  • 1/4 cup flour 1 Tsp baking powder
  • Salt n pepper
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 3 eggs
  • 2 Tbsp. milk/ water

  1. Mix spinach, flour and baking powder together at this point add extra vegetables if you would like keeping in mind you may need to add extra flour as all vegetables should be coated in flour, add salt and pepper and paprika.
  2. In a bowl mix together eggs and milk add the liquid to the flour mixture, ( should be a wet pancake like mix. )
  3. Heat pan, cook each patty until golden

Sesame Chicken

  • 250g chicken, cut into strips
  • 2 Tbsp. Soy Sauce
  • 2 Tbsp. Maple syrup
  • ¼ cup water
  • 1 Tbsp. Sesame oil
  • 1 tsp garlic minced
  • ¼ cup red pepper sliced
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • 1 tsp cornflour
  • Sesame seeds for garnish

  1. In a hot pan fry of the chicken strips, whilst cooking mix cornflour with water set aside.
  2. Put the remaining ingredients into the pan.
  3. Bring to the boil and simmer for 5 minutes then add in the cornflour mixture, continue to stir until the sauce thickens around the meat.
  4. Remove from heat and garnish with sesame seeds


Cumin Chicken on Black beans

  • 2 Chicken breasts sliced
  • ½ Tsp each cumin, cayenne pepper
  • 2 Tbsp. olive oil
  • 1 red onion diced
  • ¼ cup sliced jalapenos
  • cup black beans cooked and rinsed

  1. Combine the cumin with the cayenne and rub over the chicken.
  2. Sauté the chicken until cooked.
  3. Add the onion and jalapeño.
  4. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute.
  5. Add the beans, and 3 tablespoons water and cook stirring, 1 or 2 minutes until just heated through.
  6. Remove from heat. Season to taste with salt and black pepper.
  7. Slice the chicken and arrange on top of the beans.


Protein Balls Recipe

  • 2 scoops Protein Powder

  • 5 Tbsp. Coconut oil (melted)
  • ¼ cup dried Apricots

  1. Put all ingredients except water into a blender,
  2. blitz until at desired consistency add water if you like a wet mix,
  3. roll into 12 balls,
  4. keep refrigerated

Grilled Beef and Mango Salad

  • 1 cup baby spinach
  • 200 grams steak
  • ½ can, sliced Mango (or fresh sliced)
  • 40 grams Feta cheese crumbled

  1. Grill steak until it reaches desired doneness, flipping once. Let rest 10 minutes.
  2. Grill sliced peaches until warmed, but not mushy, while the steak is resting.
  3. Slice steak thinly against the grain. Assemble salad by adding spinach to a bowl, top with steak, peaches, and feta.
  4. Drizzle with vinaigrette to taste.
Vinaigrette
  1. Combine ½ cup of white wine vinegar with 1 Tsp sugar, ½ Tsp mustard and whisk together
  2. Slowly pour in ¼ cup oil or until mixture is thicker.
