High Protein Meals

Protein serves two main purposes in our bodies. It is essential for the growth and maintenance of the body, and any left-over protein will be used as an energy source. Protein is made up of amino acids and your body needs 20 different types, to build and maintain all the crazy enzyme’s, hormones, tissues and muscles in our bodies. However, 9 of these the body cannot produce from other amino acids and we must have them in our diet (these are known as Essential Amino Acids).

Recipes high in essential amino acids

Hollandaise

  • 3 Egg yolks
  • 2 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 cup Melted Butter

In a blender whisk together the egg yolks and the lemon juice, while the blender is still mixing slowly pour the butter into the mix until it is thick.


  1. Poached eggs can be hard to master once you have you will find yourself making them all the time. An awesome breakfast dish or added to a salad for lunch they are perfect to help you top up your protein intake for the day.
  2. Heat a pan on the stove filled with water, once simmering add in white wine vinegar, about 1 Tablespoon. Turn the Heat down now, you want the water simmering only not boiling.
  3. Crack an egg into the pan, using the back of a spoon make circles in the water to create a whirlpool, then crack in the other eggs if you are after more. Watch the eggs cook and remove from pan when at desired softness.
  4. Serve Eggs with spinach and Hollandaise.


Bean Dip

  • 1 can of beans, drained & rinsed
  • ½ onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 Tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp each cumin, paprika, cayenne pepper

  1. In a pan heat onion, garlic, oil and spices until onion is cooked
  2. Add the beans and heat through.
  3. Blend together, serve hot or cold.
