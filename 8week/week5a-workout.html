At Home Workout

Below are 3 workouts for you to try out. Start out at 30 seconds on and 30 off, and then increase your work time to 40 seconds 20 off.


Warm up

Dynamic stretches 20 seconds each.

Dislocations, overhead squat pulses, pulse lunges, chest stretch, overhead triceps, marching, but kicks, side march, lower back, core, and neck.


Upper body

Starting off with body weight exercises. The press up, triceps dips, and burpees. Then I add weight if you donâ€™t have weights at home use cans of soup or water bottles for lateral raise, shoulder press, front raise, bicep curls, bent over row and upright row.


Lower body

Starting off with the squat, curtsy lunge, frog hops, reverse lunge to high knees, tuck jumps, reverse lunge pulse, and overhead squat.

Core

Starting off with bicycle crunch, foot tap crunch, in and out crunch, flutter kicks, lying leg raises, Russian twists, and a plank.

If you are unsure about what exercises you can do or how to do them please let me know so I can either show you or talk you through them.

Cool down

Static Stretches hold for at least 30 seconds each.

Shoulders, Chest, Triceps, Hamstrings, Quadriceps, Core, Lower back, Upper back and shoulders, Lower back and Core.
