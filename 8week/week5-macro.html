The 3 Macronutrients are Carbohydrate, Protein, and Fat! Our bodies need all three for energy and to properly function.

Calorie counting is a great place to start, to learn your behaviors with food, before long you will just be able to look at something and just know what it contains maybe only going back to the basics occasionally.

Often this means playing around with numbers to suit you more specifically and get it right.
