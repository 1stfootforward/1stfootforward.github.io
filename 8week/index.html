Carbohydrates

Week 6

Why you need Carbohydrates in your diet.

When you want to slim down or just eat healthier in general, it’s tempting to try to ditch the carbs entirely. I did this myself when I first started to try and lose weight.

But here’s the thing: You need carbs (the good, healthy ones, at least). Your body depends on them to function properly.

Carbohydrates provide energy in the form of glucose or blood sugar. Glucose is necessary to support your body's everyday needs and performance. But not just any old carb will do; choosing wisely among starches, sugars and fibers helps you maintain your energy levels, avoid weight gain and combat sugar crashes.

Fat

Week 8

Fat is a macro-nutrient so, therefore, a nutrient that provides calories or energy to our bodies. The amount of energy provided by the macro-nutrients varies, fat has 9 calories per gram, more than twice the number of calories in carbs and protein, which each have 4 calories per gram.
