Food Tracking

Daily Nutrition Log

Use this food tracking Diary to record what you are eating then how you are feeling after you have eaten. Were you starving before your next scheduled meal, or did you feel sick because you ate too much?

All of this things can contribute to your goal. Include everything that goes into your mouth including water and drinks even the crusts of your child's sandwiches. I won't be tracking this information just yet just discussing it as you start to implement it in your life. Use it and ask questions as in a few week I will be tracking your food tracker. So now is a good time to get in some practice.
