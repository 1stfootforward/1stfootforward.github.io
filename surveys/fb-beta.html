Group Class Survey

Finding time these days can be hard, so fitting in time to train can be a real struggle for some especially if you are juggling a job, family and other commitments. One of the main reasons I started 1st Foot Forward is to help Women who lead busy lives to have an opportunity to train in a flexible environment. So I have made the survey below to help me help you more.





Group Classes

S.E.T: Strength and Endurance training using a barbell as resistance and utilizing the squat, press and compound lifts you will work your entire body gaining Strength and Endurance.

Hiit Camp: High Intensity Interval Training, burn calories fast. Perfect class for those short on time.

Just Stretch: Yoga inspired, movement prep class. Gain flexibility, strength and confidence as you work your body through different moves to improve posture and how your body functions.

Beginners Box / Kick-fit: Learn the fundamentals and correct technique whilst you build Strength and Confidence.
